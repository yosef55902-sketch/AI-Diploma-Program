# Course Schedule | الجدول الزمني للدورة

## 12-Week Course Timeline | الجدول الزمني لدورة 12 أسبوع

**Total Duration:** 12 weeks (3 months)  
**Hours per week:** 6-8 hours (2-3 hours class + 4-5 hours self-study)

---

## Week-by-Week Breakdown | التفصيل الأسبوعي

### **Week 1: Setup & Python Libraries** | الأسبوع الأول: الإعداد ومكتبات بايثون

**Learning Objectives:**
- Set up development environment
- Learn essential Python libraries for AI
- Understand NumPy, Matplotlib, Collections, heapq

**Activities:**
- Day 1-2: Installation and setup
- Day 3-5: Notebook 00 (Python Libraries for AI)
- Day 6-7: Practice exercises

**Deliverables:**
- Environment setup complete
- Notebook 00 completed
- Quiz 00 (end of week)

**Assessment:**
- Quiz 00: Python Libraries (5% of grade)

---

### **Week 2: Search Algorithms** | الأسبوع الثاني: خوارزميات البحث

**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand graph concepts
- Implement BFS, DFS, A*, Dijkstra
- Apply search algorithms to problems

**Activities:**
- Day 1-3: Notebook 01 (Search Algorithms)
- Day 4-5: Practice problems
- Day 6-7: Algorithm visualization exercises

**Deliverables:**
- Notebook 01 completed
- Practice exercises submitted
- Quiz 01 (end of week)

**Assessment:**
- Quiz 01: Search Algorithms (10% of grade)

---

### **Week 3: Knowledge Representation** | الأسبوع الثالث: تمثيل المعرفة

**Learning Objectives:**

**Activities:**
- Day 1-3: Notebook 02 (Knowledge Representation)

- Day 6-7: Build simple knowledge graph

**Deliverables:**
- Notebook 02 completed
- Knowledge graph exercise
- Quiz 02 (end of week)

**Assessment:**
- Quiz 02: Knowledge Representation (10% of grade)

---

**Deliverables:**

- Quiz 03 (end of week)

**Assessment:**
- Quiz 03: Uncertainty (10% of grade)

### **Week 5: Optimization Techniques** | الأسبوع الخامس: تقنيات التحسين

### **Week 7-8: Project Development** | الأسبوع السابع والثامن: تطوير المشروع

## Flexible Schedule Options | خيارات الجدول المرن

### **Accelerated (8 weeks):**
- Combine weeks 1-2
- Combine weeks 3-4
- Skip Week 9 (review)
- Skip Week 10 (advanced topics)

### **Extended (16 weeks):**
- Add more practice time
- Include Week 10 (advanced topics)
- Add additional projects
- More review sessions

### **Part-time (24 weeks):**
- Half the weekly hours
- Spread over 6 months
- More self-paced
- Extended project time
